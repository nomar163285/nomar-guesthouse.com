Concept

An easy place to stay — for hikers, and for anyone taking a break on their journey.

Just a 3-minute walk from the station — an easy stop for hikers and travelers alike.

  • 🚏3 minutes on foot from the station/bus terminal. Direct highway buses from Shinjuku, Nagoya, Osaka and Kyoto.
  • 🍜A shopping street right nearby — explore local restaurants and take your time on your journey.
  • 🎒Dorm beds from ¥3,500/night. A relaxed, no-frills base to sort your gear before heading into the mountains.

Area Guide

Around the Area

[Photo placeholder] Sauce katsudon

Sauce Katsudon

Komagane's signature dish — sauce katsudon. Enjoy it at well-known shops in town such as Meijitei and Tonkatsu Kiraku.

Find a restaurant (Komagane Chamber of Commerce)
[Photo placeholder] Senjojiki Cirque view

Senjojiki Cirque

A bowl-shaped landform carved by glaciers around 20,000 years ago. Breathtaking views from 2,612m. The loop trail takes about 45 minutes, and the scenery changes with every season.

Official website
[Photo placeholder] Kozenji Temple grounds

Kozenji Temple

An old temple linked to the legend of the sacred dog Hayataro. The weeping cherry blossom light-up in April is especially famous.

Official website
[Photo placeholder] Hayataro Onsen hot spring

Hayataro Onsen

A hot spring area with 3 day-use baths and 9 lodging facilities. A clear, alkaline simple hot spring known as a "beauty bath."

Official website
[Photo placeholder] Komagane station-front shopping street

Komagane Station-front Shopping Street

A nostalgic shopping street where you can chat with locals or take retro-style photos. Komagane is also a rare town producing beer, wine, whisky, and sake all at once.

Official website

Also try: horse sashimi (a local specialty of the Ina Valley), and coarsely-ground soba noodles (found at notable shops in the Komagane Highland area).

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is parking available?

Yes. It's about a 1-minute walk from the guesthouse and costs ¥500 per stay. Limited to 3 cars. If full, please use a nearby coin parking lot.

Do you have a female-only dormitory?

Yes, we have a female-only dormitory with 4 beds.

Can you store luggage before check-in or after check-out?

Yes, this is possible. Please contact us in advance to arrange the timing.

What is the rate for children?

Children aged 5 and under stay free when sharing a bed with a parent.

Is Wi-Fi free?

Yes, it's free.
