Check Availability About the Guesthouse
Concept

A budget stay. The "LCC of guesthouses" for hikers.

We built a budget guesthouse in Komagane.

For people heading into the mountains, and for those just passing through — we wanted to give travelers one more option for "where to stay." Until now, Komagane didn't have an affordable guesthouse.

There's a shopping street right near us. Take your time, stop by the local restaurants, and enjoy the journey.

We're proud of our access: direct highway buses from Shinjuku, Nagoya, Osaka and Kyoto to Komagane Bus Terminal, and just a 3-minute walk from the station/terminal to the guesthouse.

🏔️ Sightseeing
[Photo placeholder] Senjojiki Cirque view

Senjojiki Cirque

A bowl-shaped landform carved by glaciers around 20,000 years ago. Breathtaking views from the 2,612m Senjojiki Station; a roughly 45-minute loop trail. The scenery changes with every season.

Kozenji Temple

An old temple linked to the legend of the "loyal dog Hayataro." The weeping cherry blossom light-up in April is especially famous.

Hayataro Onsen

A hot spring area with 3 day-use baths and 9 lodging facilities. A clear, alkaline simple hot spring known as a "beauty bath."
